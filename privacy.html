Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

NeuroDigest is a free weekly neurology newsletter curated by Vincenzo Laterza, MD, Neurologist at Di Venere Hospital, Bari, Italy. This page explains how we collect and use your data.

What we collect

We collect only your email address when you subscribe to the newsletter. We do not collect your name, location, or any other personal information unless you voluntarily provide it by contacting us.

How we use your data

Your email address is used exclusively to send you the weekly NeuroDigest newsletter. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

Third-party services

We use Resend (resend.com) to deliver emails. Your email address is stored on their servers in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed. When you unsubscribe, your address is permanently removed from our mailing list within 24 hours.

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also withdraw your consent to receive emails at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@neuro-digest.com.

Unsubscribe

Every newsletter includes a one-click unsubscribe link at the bottom. You can also unsubscribe at any time by emailing us at info@neuro-digest.com.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts. No cookies are set when you visit neuro-digest.com.
